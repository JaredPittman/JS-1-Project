Minecraft Information Board

Minecraft is a popular video game that was created by Markus Persson and released by Mojang in 2011. The game allows players to build and explore virtual worlds made up of blocks. Players can mine for resources, craft items, and create structures, as well as battle monsters and other players. The game has a variety of different game modes, including survival mode, creative mode, and adventure mode. It is available on a variety of platforms, including PC, consoles, and mobile devices.

Terraria Information Board

Terraria is a popular video game that was released in 2011 by the game studio Re-Logic. It is a 2D sandbox game that is similar to Minecraft, in that it allows players to explore, build, and survive in a virtual world made up of blocks. However, Terraria also has elements of action and adventure, and features a variety of enemies and boss fights. The game has both single-player and multiplayer modes, and is available on a variety of platforms, including PC, consoles, and mobile devices.
